CNI - National Confederation of Industry

The National Confederation of Industry (CNI) is the highest organization of the Brazilian industrial sector. It coordinates a system formed by 27 industry federations from the states and the Federal District - to which 1,016 employers' unions are affiliated - and manages the National Service for Industrial Learning (SENAI), the Social Service for Industry (SESI) and the Euvaldo Lodi Institute ( IEL).

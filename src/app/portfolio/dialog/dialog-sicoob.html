Sicoob - Credit Cooperative System

The Credit Cooperative System (Sicoob) is the largest cooperative financial system in Brazil with more than 4.8 million members, 2.8 thousand service points, distributed throughout the country. It is made up of financial cooperatives and support companies, which together offer the members of the cooperative services of current account, credit, investment, cards, social security, consortium, insurance, bank collection, acquisition of electronic means of payment, among others.
Company: Mirante Tecnologia
Period: 04/2012 - 08/2014

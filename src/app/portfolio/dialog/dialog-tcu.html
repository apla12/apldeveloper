Federal Court of Accounts - TCU Brazil

TCU is the federal government's external control body and assists the National Congress in the mission of monitoring the country's budgetary and financial execution and contributing to the improvement of Public Administration for the benefit of society. To this end, it aims to be a reference in the promotion of an effective, ethical, agile and responsible Public Administration.
